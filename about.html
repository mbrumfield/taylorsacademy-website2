Meet the Dream Team!

Ms. Conisha Hackett, Director

The architect of Taylor's Academy of Excellence, Conisha Hackett, has always had a dream of one day opening her own summer educational academy. She began working on this endeavor last March by hosting 8 students at her grandmother's home in Summit, Mississippi. The two day workshop featured courses such as "How to Ace the ACT," "Knowing the Institution: the Difference Between HBCU's and PWI's," "Higher Ed with Lower Costs: Scholarships and Financial Aid," and "Coloring Colleges: What it Means to be Black in Higher Education." On the second day she took the students on a tour of thee Historic Tougaloo College. Attending the induction ceremony of Alpha Lambda Delta, a freshman honor society, eating in the on-campus cafeteria, and mingling with the college students, the youths participating gained a firsthand experience of college life.

Earning her Bachelors of Arts in History from Tougaloo College, Conisha is currently enrolled in the Masters program in History at Mississippi State University in Starkville, Mississippi. She has had the opportunity to enhance her teaching resume through McComb School District's summer Children's Defense Fund freedom school program as well as her current appointment as a Teaching Assistant for the Department of History at Mississippi State University.

Ms. Skyler Morgan

During the early stages of graduate school at Mississippi State, Skyler Morgan met Ms. Hackett and they quickly learned they shared very similar goals. Ms. Morgan also had dreams of one day opening a series of interactive political science academies to prepare future minority leaders for possible careers in politics. These efforts stemmed from a growing concern of the lack of minority participation in the political spectrum. The academy will feature performances that range from mock trials, presidential debates, historical remixes to popular songs, and skits.

Her educational credentials include a Bachelors of Science in Social Sciences Education from Alabama State University. Skyler is currently enrolled in the Master's in History program at Mississippi State University in Starkville, Mississippi. Her teaching credentials include her Spring internship at Brewbaker Technology Magnet School in Montgomery, Alabama. She is also currently a Teaching Assistant for the Department of History at Mississippi State University.
