Engaging Classroom Strategies for Building Excellent Scholars

Mission Statement

At Taylor's Academy of Excellence we promote the dignity and self-worth of all of our Excellence Scholars, and strive to train them to become critical thinkers capable of analytical reasoning. To that end, we encourage scholars' decision-making process through the Excellence Mentors, access to research materials relative to their career goals, and self-determination in activities, socialization, and life and career planning strategies.

Each classroom is equipped with the necessary tools for an interactive learning environment: an energetic teacher and the student's imagination.

Features - What does TAE offer?.

Roots to Identity: African American History

This exciting course covers topics from the partition of Africa, the Middle Passage, and the Long Civil Rights Movement.

Mathematics

This interactive course provides a review of math concepts from subjects such as Algebra and Geometry. The students will complete various worksheets and activities to immerse themselves in a commonly troubling subject for youth.
