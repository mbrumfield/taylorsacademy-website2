Investment Opportunity

This summer program is designed to enlighten students from low income backgrounds in order to break the financial and academic barriers that restrict public school students to broad scope of education. These early efforts to provide a relevant curriculum to identify and explore uncharted narratives in history to lead to the student's self identification will be the foundation of our future work in local communities.

The cost for this program per youth is totaled at $75.00. This cost includes a $50 application and registration fee and a $25 field trip fee (The $25 field trip fee can be raised during our Excellence fundraiser or the parents can choose to pay the additional cost).

In order to raise money for these ambitious efforts, we are offering four investment opportunities: a GoFundMe account, a tax credit donation option, advertisement opportunities, and a fundraiser led by the Excellence mentors during the program. All three of these fundraising opportunities will go directly to the program's budget.

Personal Donations

We fully understand the busy schedules we all face. For your convenience, we have a GoFundMe account for personal donations.
